# Netherlands

## Strategic Summary
**"Order, light, modern urban living."**

## Identity and Sensations
The local page experience for the Netherlands adopts a stance of immaculate order and pragmatic contemporary living under profound sophistication. It immediately conveys:
- Absolute visual clarity
- Serenity and cleanliness
- Geometric and mathematical rigor
- A strong connection to and capture of light

## Parameters of Local Aesthetics
- **Base Visual Inspiration:** Dutch metropolitan architecture, the grid of the canals, massive glass panels, windows that dissolve boundaries, and highly meticulous urban planning.
- **Modulated Color Palette:** 
  - Icy and cold whites
  - Fog gray tones
  - Deep urban blue
  - Muted or broken greens
  - Discreet, sporadic terracotta accents
- **Typography and Composition (Layout):** The design strongly embraces emptiness and balanced contrast through immovable grids. Abundance of "air" (white space). It offers a structured, predictable, and wonderfully efficient rhythm.

## Promise and Local Catalog (Hero Assets)
- Light-filled contemporary apartments in avid districts of Amsterdam.
- The classic and elegant renovated row houses ("townhouses"). 
- Residential concepts where technological sustainability and comfort converge in modern family home design.
