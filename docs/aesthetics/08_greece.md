# Greece

## Strategic Summary
**"Radiant depth, ancient warmth, and pure coastal living."**

## Identity and Sensations
The section devoted to Greece balances monumental architectural heritage with the absolute purity of Mediterranean island life. Entering this embassy evokes:
- Blinding, radiant natural light
- A profound connection to ancient history
- Absolute purity and coastal minimalism
- Timeless warmth

## Parameters of Local Aesthetics
- **Base Visual Inspiration:** The cradle of Western architecture. Highlights include the immortal columns of Athens, the pure Cycladic whitewashed walls of the islands, bare, sun-baked stone, open terraces overlooking the sea, and the overwhelming contrast between white structures and the deep Aegean blue.
- **Modulated Color Palette:** 
  - Cycladic Whitewash
  - Sun-baked Stone / Light Sand
  - Deep Monumental Gray
  - Aegean Sea Blue
  - Olive branch greens as subtle accents
- **Typography and Composition (Layout):** A layout that breathes classicism. Uses extremely elegant, carved-feeling display typography alongside large expanses of bright white space. Photography emphasizes stark contrasts between light and shadow, and the deep blues of the sea and sky.

## Promise and Local Catalog (Hero Assets)
- Historical Athenian residences blending mythological heritage with hyper-modern luxury.
- Iconic coastal villas and island retreats featuring seamless indoor-outdoor living.
- Penthouses and estates highlighting monumental layouts and pure, uninterrupted sea views.
