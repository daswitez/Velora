# Aesthetics & Colors: Main Landing Page

## The Neutral Canvas
The aesthetic rule here is **Total Neutrality**. The Main Landing acts as the art gallery's white walls. The colorful "art" is the 8 countries. 

## The Palette
- **Base (Background):** Pure Silence (`#FFFFFF` to `#FDFDFD`). 
- **Surface (Cards/Overlays):** Translucent White (Glassmorphism) or pure `#F5F5F5` to create subtle depth without color.
- **Primary Text:** Anthracite Black (`#0A0A0A`).
- **Accent:** Pure Silver/Gray. There is **no vibrant accent color** on this page. Wait until the user enters a country for color to explode.

## Photographic Direction
- We use overarching European architectural photography. 
- **Tone:** Slightly desaturated, high contrast, playing heavily with natural light and deep shadows (chiaroscuro effect).
- **Subject:** Details (a massive marble staircase, a single window looking out at the ocean, a modern concrete pillar). Avoid cliché wide-shots.

## Shapefest / 3D Usage
- If 3D shapes are used, they must be pure frosted glass or white marble. They act as invisible structural elements shifting in the background.
